To get involved with the Radicle project, start by joining our community channels:

Join our Matrix chat

For real-time discussion on everything related to Radicle

The best way to join our chat is by making an account on matrix.radicle.community and joining our community homepage. This is where you can find a list of our available rooms. The address for our homeserver is matrix.radicle.xyz.
